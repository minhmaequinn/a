Website Links

 
The following are some external resources.
 

Wikipedia
The free encyclopedia that anyone can edit.

The Internet Movie Database
Movie plot summaries, actors biographies.

Ebay
Buy and sell anything. The world's largest garage sale.

Amazon
Books, movies, music and everything else you need.

Square Peach Music
Royalty free mp3 music for website and media projects.

Biddingfortravel
"Save money on travel with the leading resource for informed priceline bidding". We use this site to check for lowest available hotel prices on Priceline.

Priceline
After finding out the hotel price from Biddingfortravel I book my hotel stay via this website.

Orbitz
We normally use this site to get an idea of the hotel, car and airplane prices. To save even more money, after finding out which airline has the flights I want, I then go to the actual airline's website and make booking there. Major Airline Links: American Airlines, United Airlines, Delta Airlines, America West & US Airways, Continental Airlines, Nortwest Airlines.

Hotwire
Another site that you can use to get an rough idea of the hotel, car and or  airplane prices (I tend to prefer Orbitz over this site). 

Carrentals
We usually book our car rentals at this place. The pricing is pretty competitive here.



Note: I you have a link and would like to add to this list, please let us know.
